About this project

The FfD Country Scorecard is a structured diagnostic tool scoring countries across 25 indicators in five pillars — Flows, Expenditure, Spending Effectiveness, Enabling Environment, and Financial Market Diagnostics — on a 1–5 scale against fixed international benchmarks.

It is designed as a conversation starter for donor–government dialogue, not an official assessment or a cross-country ranking tool. The project is currently a prototype.
