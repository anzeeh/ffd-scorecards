# ffd-scorecards
Financing for development - Country scorecards
A diagnostic tool that translates development-finance indicators into structured, visual country
assessments — built to support dialogue on financing for development priorities.

Each scorecard scores a country across **25 indicators in 5 pillars**:

| Pillar | Focus |
|---|---|
| I. Flows | Domestic revenue, FDI, ODA, remittances, debt |
| II. Expenditure | Sectoral spending (education, health, social protection), fiscal space |
| III. Spending Effectiveness | PFM quality, governance, banking health, aid effectiveness |
| IV. Enabling Environment | Investment climate, rule of law, transparency, INFF/AML frameworks |
| V. Financial Market Diagnostics | Credit access, banking soundness, sovereign risk |

Indicators are scored 1–5 against fixed international benchmarks. Full scoring logic, sourcing
rules, and known limitations are documented in **[METHODOLOGY.md](./METHODOLOGY.md)** — please
read that before citing or reusing any score.

## What this is — and isn't

- ✅ A **structured conversation starter** for  dialogue on financing priorities.
- ✅ Built from publicly available international data sources, with sourcing shown on every card.
- ❌ **Not** an official government or institutional assessment.
- ❌ **Not** a cross-country ranking tool — pillar weightings and thresholds are designed for
  within-country diagnostic use, not country-to-country comparison.
- ❌ **Not** a substitute for primary-source verification before use in formal reporting.

## Status

This is a **prototype** covering some countries. The methodology and toolchain will be refined with the goal of institutionalizing the approach
across a wider set of country operations.
